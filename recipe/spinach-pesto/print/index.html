Ingredients
  • 1cup baby spinach
  • 1/4cup basil leaves
  • 1/8 tsp red chilli flakesOptional
  • 2-3cloves garlic
  • 2tablespoons walnutstoasted
  • 1tablespoon lemon juice
  • 3tablespoons olive oil
  • salt and pepperto taste
Instructions
  1. Pulse everything except the olive oil in your food processor. With the motor on, stream the olive oil through the feed tube till the pesto comes together. Store in an airtight container with a thin film of olive oil over it.
